About Me

Hi, I'm Hayley. I am a qualified Person-Centred counsellor and reflexologist, offering services in both individually. I have a passion for helping people, whether we're working through issues in counselling, or healing the body in a reflexology session.

Within my counselling practice, I offer face-to-face therapy in Rochford, telephone counselling and walk and talk therapy. I am a member of the British Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy (BACP) and fully insured with Towergate. I also have an enhanced DBS.

As a reflexologist, I offer a tailored, alternate means of healing to address everyone's individual specific needs. I pinpoint areas on the feet where I can apply pressure to stimulate healing energy, which flows through the body's energetic pathways to the targeted areas of the body.
